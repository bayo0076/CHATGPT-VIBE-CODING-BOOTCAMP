About Adebayo

A newsroom-trained operator who builds real things across borders

Adebayo Olawunmi has spent over fifteen years reporting for the News Agency of Nigeria, covering the stories that shape policy, business and public life across Lagos and beyond. That same discipline — verify first, communicate clearly, move fast — now drives his work as a business strategist, connecting Nigerian opportunity to international capital, education and trade.

He leads Bee-Moore Advisory, an FDI and market-entry consulting firm; co-founded and directs Concerned Nation Builders Initiative, an NGO delivering development projects across five Local Government Areas in Lagos State's Epe Division; and serves as Co-Founder and Country Director Nigeria for Fovare, an edtech platform expanding access to quality education across emerging markets.

Most recently, he has been applying AI-assisted, "vibe coding" workflows to build and validate software products quickly — pairing a journalist's instinct for what a market actually needs with a builder's bias for shipping.

"Every good story and every good deal start the same way — you go where the facts are, and you don't leave until you understand them."
